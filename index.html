The Certified Great On Their Behalf Practitioner Credential

Certified Great On Their Behalf Practitioners are trained practitioners who deliver the Great On Their Behalf governance framework to school boards. Unlike trainers who teach content, practitioners guide boards through a structured process of self-assessment, reflection, and commitment to outcomes-focused governance.

  • Certified by Effective School Boards, LLC
  • Trained in the GOTB five-practice framework
  • Authorized to deliver the GOTB Index to school districts

What a Certified Great On Their Behalf Practitioner Does

Assess

Guide boards through the GOTB Index self-assessment process, helping members honestly evaluate their current governance practices.

Facilitate

Lead structured governance conversations that move boards from awareness to committed behavior change.

Monitor

Support boards in establishing monitoring systems that track student outcome progress over time.

Practitioner vs. Trainer vs. Consultant

Trainer

Delivers content

Consultant

Provides answers

Practitioner

Creates conditions for lasting change

Certified Great On Their Behalf Practitioners don't tell boards what to do. They create the conditions under which boards discover what they need to do — and commit to doing it.
